Why I Built This Site

This website began as a school project ‚Äî but for me, it's more than that. It's my way of giving back to a place that shaped who I am. Through smooth animations, interactive design, and a hint of retro charm, I wanted to make exploring Rizal feel magical and meaningful.

Each town page was carefully crafted to showcase not just the landmarks, but the soul of these communities. The carousel effects, bento grid layouts, and responsive design all serve one purpose: to create an immersive experience that does justice to Rizal's beauty.

How I Made It

Building Explore Rizal Province was a labor of love that combined multiple disciplines:

  • Frontend Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (with custom animations for the carousel and bento grid)
  • Design: Photoshop & Illustrator for graphics and layout concepts
  • Research: Documenting each town's history, culture, and landmarks
  • Countless hours of coding, debugging, and testing across devices

The most challenging (and rewarding) part was creating the interactive town pages with their unique scrolling effects and content reveals. I wanted visitors to feel like they're discovering each location naturally, just like when you explore in real life.
